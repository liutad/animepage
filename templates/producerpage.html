The Eminence in Shadow

An anime television series adaptation was announced on the fourth volume of the light novel on February 26, 2021. The series is produced by Nexus.

Producer

Director: Kazuya Nakanishi

Scriptwriter: Kanichi Katou

Character designer: Makoto Iino

Music: Kenichiro Suehiro

Season 1

The opening theme is "Highest" by OxT while the ending theme is "Darling in the Night" by the Shadow Garden cast.

Season 2

The opening theme is "Grayscale Dominator" by OxT while the ending theme is "Polaris in the Night" by Ikumi Hasegawa and others.
